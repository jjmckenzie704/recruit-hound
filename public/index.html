Chad Pilker has served in a variety of roles in both Corporate America and in the U.S. Armed Forces. Following an eight-year stint as an Infantry Officer where he served as a Platoon Leader through Company Commander, Chad hung up his boots, moved to Charlotte, and joined Bank of America as a Business Continuity Consultant. It was at BoA where Chad learned the power of technology and the necessity of it in business. It was at a Coding Bootcamp where he and a group of his peers had the idea to form Recruit Hound to aid those looking for jobs, but not knowing where to start. Chad is passionate about serving others and continues to do so.
